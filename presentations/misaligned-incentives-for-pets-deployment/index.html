Lost in Translation: When Scientists, Regulators, and Industry Walk into a Bar... and Nobody Gets It.

Incentives ≠ Aligned

This leads to (at least) three kinds of badness

  1. Technical solutions that are not actually implementable
  2. Legal interpretations that don’t survive contact with reality
  3. Social solutions that deeply misunderstand the incentives of the various actors
